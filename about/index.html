Who Am I?

Hello, my name is Ellis Berner. I craft rich internet applications and damn fine pixels. By day I am en engineer at DocuSign in the Greater Seattle Area. At night, I scour the world for beauty in type, color and design. For me, it was never about playing the game, but designing the map.

Music is a true passion of mine. I like all kinds, especially String Cheese Incident, The Doors, and The Who. I am also a big fan of directors Richard Linklater, Werner Herzog and Fritz Lang. My favorite game is Go, for its complex simplicity. I rank internationally as 11 kyu against GNU Go. If you would like to let me know of some projects you need help with, you have found an error, or you simply want to chat then send me a message.
